Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 10, 2026

BearAlign does not directly collect or store users’ personal information. This app does not require account registration, login, name, email address, contact information, location information, health information, or any other information that can identify an individual.

1. Personal Information We Collect

BearAlign does not collect personal information.

2. Information Stored in the App

Settings required for app use, such as reminder times, alarm sounds, vibration settings, exercise preferences, and completion records, may be stored locally on the user’s device. This information is not transmitted to any external server.

3. Notification Permission

BearAlign may request notification permission from the device in order to provide stretching reminders. Notification permission can be changed or withdrawn at any time in the device settings.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

BearAlign may use Google AdMob advertisements to support app operations. In this process, advertising providers may process non-identifying information such as device information and advertising identifiers, in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

5. Sharing Personal Information with Third Parties

BearAlign does not directly collect users’ personal information and therefore does not share such information with third parties.

6. Retention and Deletion of Personal Information

BearAlign does not directly retain personal information. App settings and records are stored locally on the device and may be deleted when the app is uninstalled.

7. User Rights

Users can change notification permissions in the device settings and delete locally stored app data by uninstalling the app.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated due to changes in applicable laws or service features. Any changes will be announced through the app or webpage.
